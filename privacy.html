Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

The National Government Contract Center ("NGCC") is operated by Apropos Group LLC. This Privacy Policy explains, in general terms, how information may be collected, used, stored, and protected when you use NGCC websites, onboarding flows, dashboards, contract-intelligence tools, support channels, and related services.

Information We May Collect

Depending on how you use NGCC, we may collect information such as:

  • Name, business name, business email address, contact information, and account identifiers.
  • Business capability information, industry information, NAICS codes, certifications, service areas, contracting interests, and other profile information used to support opportunity matching.
  • Information you submit through onboarding, account, support, assessment, capability, proposal-development, or other service workflows.
  • Account-access and security information, including one-time-code requests, verification events, session information, and related authentication records.
  • Technical information reasonably necessary to operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the website and services.

How We Use Information

We may use information to provide and operate NGCC services; create and maintain user access; match businesses with government contracting opportunities; generate business, capability, opportunity, or proposal-related outputs requested by users; provide support; improve service quality; prevent misuse; maintain security; and comply with applicable obligations.

Government and Public-Record Data

NGCC may display or process information obtained from government systems, procurement portals, public records, or other public sources. Public-source information may be combined with information supplied by a user to support research, matching, scoring, organization, and workflow functions.

Service Providers

We may use service providers for hosting, databases, authentication, email delivery, analytics, security, document processing, artificial-intelligence services, and related infrastructure. These providers may process information only as needed to provide services to us, subject to their applicable agreements and controls.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information as part of NGCC's ordinary service model. We may disclose information to service providers that support NGCC, when directed or authorized by the user, when reasonably necessary to protect the service or its users, or when required by law or valid legal process.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and operational safeguards designed to protect information. No online system can guarantee absolute security, and users are responsible for protecting access to their own email accounts, devices, and credentials.

Data Retention

Information may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain business and security records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet applicable legal or operational requirements.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit optional information. Some information may be required to provide account access, business matching, or requested services. Requests concerning account or personal information should be directed to Apropos Group LLC through the official contact channel provided for NGCC.

Children's Privacy

NGCC is designed for business and professional use and is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as NGCC services evolve. The "Last updated" date will be revised when material changes are published.

Contact

For privacy questions concerning NGCC, contact Apropos Group LLC through the official contact information provided on the NGCC website or your NGCC account communications.

Important: NGCC is a private business service operated by Apropos Group LLC and is not a government agency.
